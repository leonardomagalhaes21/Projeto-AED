\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Functor for comparing students by their names in descending order. 

@detail This struct provides a custom comparison operator to compare students by their names. It is designed for use with standard sorting algorithms like std\+::sort to sort students in descending order of their names.

Time Complexity\+: O(n \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} log(n)), where \textquotesingle{}n\textquotesingle{} is the number of students being sorted.
